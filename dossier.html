🔍 Apartment Hunting Checklist (8 things to check per listing)
  1. Walk time to YOUR subway station — over 8 min = skip. This matters more than rent.
  2. 11 PM commute — check Google Maps at 11 PM, not just morning. Trains go local at night.
  3. Bus-dependent or subway-only? — if Google routes via bus, you're too far from the subway.
  4. Which floor? Walk-up or elevator? — 5th floor walk-up at 11 PM with groceries = misery.
  5. Actual square footage — listings often omit or have errors. Verify in person.
  6. Laundry: in-unit, basement, or laundromat? — if none, budget $35/mo for wash-and-fold service.
  7. Owner vs broker listing — owner = more negotiable. FARE Act = no broker fee in NYC.
  8. What's between you and the station at night? — well-lit commercial street or dark residential blocks?
